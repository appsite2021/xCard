What is xCard?

A xCard is a loyalty app that digitises traditional punch cards. It enables customers to collect stamps, points, redeem rewards via their smartphones, all without any physical contact.

How are stamps and
Points collected?

Stamps and points can be collected through various actions or transactions, depending on the loyalty program's design, eg.

Purchase-Based: Customers earn stamps or points based on the amount they spend. For instance, a coffee shop might offer one stamp for every coffee purchased, and after collecting five stamps, the customer earns a free drink.

Frequency-Based: Customers earn stamps or points based on the number of visits or transactions. For example, a sandwich shop might offer a stamp for each visit, and after collecting ten stamps, the customer receives a discount on their next purchase.
